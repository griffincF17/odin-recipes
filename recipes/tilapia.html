Pan-seared Tilapia

Description

A popular and delicious seafood dish that is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Tilapia fillet(s)
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted melted butter

Steps

  1. Rinse fillet(s) in cold water and season.
  2. Gently coat fillet(s) in flour.
  3. Heat olive oil and cook fillets for about 4 minutes.
  4. Brush butter on fillet(s) just before cooking is finished.
