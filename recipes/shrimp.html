Seasoned Steamed Shrimp

Description

A nice, quick, and tasty seafood dish to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp seafood seasoning
  • 1 lb. shrimp

Steps

  1. Boil the water, vingear, and seasoning.
  2. Add the shrimp and stir.
  3. Reduce the heat, cover, and steam for 3-5 min.
  4. Drain the water.
